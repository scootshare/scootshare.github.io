Somerville Cooperative Trailer Share (Scoots)

Welcome! We are a small community of cycling and sustainability enthusiasts hoping to spread the use of alternative hauling options. If you want to borrow a bike trailer, check out the steps listed below. If you would like to add a trailer to our site (awesome!), please complete this form. Please be mindful that this is a program in BETA. If you have comments or questions, please send a message to biketrailershare at gmail dot com

Important information!

  1. Membership costs $10/yr to help with maintenance costs. Just bring 10 bucks when you pick up your first trailer!
  2. Be safe! All damage or accidents during a borrowing session are the responsibility of the borrower.
  3. Always treat others and the trailers with respect!

How to borrow a trailer

  1. Check out the map below to see descriptions and find a trailer near you.
  2. Click on the pin to find more information about the trailer.
  3. Send a request to borrow a trailer. Please note that each owner sets their own borrowing policy. For example, some owners may want to meet borrowers or some trailers may be locked.
